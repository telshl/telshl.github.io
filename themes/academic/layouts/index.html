Prof. Shih-Hung Lo

Media, Democracy, Platforms & Public Knowledge

研究媒體制度、新聞業危機、平台治理、事實查核與 AI 時代的公共傳播秩序。關注媒體如何維持民主社會的知識信任與公共生活。

Professor of Communication at National Chung Cheng University, Taiwan. His research spans the political economy of communication, media reform, platform governance, and journalism crisis. He co-founded the Media Reform Coalition and weReport investigative journalism platform, and served as Board Chair of the Taiwan FactCheck Center (2022–2026).
